About This Tool

This tool searches PubMed directly from your browser and stores articles in your local browser storage. All your data stays on your device.

Features:

  • Search PubMed across 21+ clinical specialty areas
  • Persistent local archive (survives browser restarts)
  • Export/import articles for backup or multi-device sync
  • Fully offline once loaded
  • No account or login required

Data Management

Export or import your article collection as JSON. Use this to back up your archive or sync across devices.

How to Use

Search: Use the search tab to query PubMed. Check the topics relevant to your interests or enter custom keywords. Results appear below.

Save Articles: Click "Save to Archive" on any result to store it permanently.

Browse Archive: View all saved articles in the Archive tab. Search and filter by title, author, or topic.

Sync Across Devices: Export your archive as JSON, then import it on another device to sync your collection.
